About Us

We are Epicodus students who's driven to learn programming. We do 100% hands-on, building projects and writing code all day, every day. Most class days, we practice pair programming: two people sharing one computer, taking turns who uses the keyboard and mouse. By working together, we catch each other's mistakes, teach each other new skills, and come up with ideas together neither of you would have had alone. Pairing is increasingly used by tech companies (like Facebook and Square), and we've found that pair programming helps us learn programming concepts faster, too.

Our Project

We've come up with an idea to build a vacation planner for people and added some more info for them to see. Everybody wants to travel around the world and explore. Even us authors would love to see the cities that we put in on our webpage someday. A number of studies have shown that taking time away from the job can have physical and psychological health benefits. People who take vacations have lower stress, less risk of heart disease, a better outlook on life, and more motivation to achieve goals.
